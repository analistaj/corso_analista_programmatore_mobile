I manage small teams in internal and external projects for private companies and public institutions.
I bring 3 years of experience as a Java Developer providing business logic and data management. Recently I started to work on Icaro, a Health care web-based integrated solution, and I'm upgrading and enriching the business logic. Prior to this, I helped to update and offer new web-based services to the Cesena Municipality and I developed ETL transformations from different data sources to a database-independent environment and implemented the Data Normalization for SCACCo, a solution for local authorities to integrate data and analysis.
As a PHP Developer, I worked mainly for building the portal of the Enterprise Sector of the Milan Municipality and other private companies' web applications and portals. I also worked in a development team designing and building in .NET the administration management of SOCIABLE, a motivating platform for elderly networking, mental reinforcement and social interaction, a pilot type B project supported by the ICT-PSP Programme of the European Commission.
